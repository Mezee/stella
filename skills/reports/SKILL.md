# Stella Reports

Use this skill for written deliverables built from Stella chart data.

## Workflow

1. Calculate the chart data first.
2. Preserve a chart-facts section, then interpretation, then validation status.
3. Keep assumptions and missing-data caveats visible.

## Doctrine

Reports synthesize existing chart data; they do not add unsupported doctrine.
